What We Offer

Yoga

Whether you’re looking for an awakening Ashtanga series or craving a Vinyasa flow, you’ll find your perfect class here. Our instructors are inspiring and bring their own flare to each session

Pilates

The low-impact exercise that will improve your posture and boost muscle strength. Perfect for anyone looking to strengthen their core, power up back muscles and tone inner and outer thighs

Meditation

Escape the hustle and bustle of city life and tune in to your own higher vibrations. We offer a range of meditation classes from guided mindfulness practices to soothing gong baths

Cardio

Get that heart pumping with our wide range of cardio blasts. HIIT, Tabata, Spin and many more. You name it, we do it. With instructors who will help you push harder, you’re sure to reach your goals

Swimming

Whatever your ability, we’ve got a swim session that’s right for you. Whether you’re looking to take it slow or you’re training for a triathlon, our state of the art pool provides the perfect arena

Weight Training

Strengthen your muscles with our targeted weight training classes. With the soul focus on building power and body shaping, these sessions are designed for everyone – ladies included!
